Constitution of the Republic of Jayditaria - 2021

Please note that this is an incomplete constitution. It will still be updated until further notice.

PREAMBLE

We, the sovereign nation of the Republic of Jayditaria, in order to establish a Republic wherein all rights of the people are guaranteed, freedom is mandatory, and choice is not an option, but compulsory, do promulgate and ratify this Constitution, to establish a nation built on liberty, equality and fraternity, and the guaranteed protection of the basic rights and freedoms of man.

ARTICLE I - NATIONAL PRINCIPLES AND GOVERNANCE

SECTION 1 - NATIONAL PRINCIPLES

Jayditaria is a federal, democratic, and republican state. All power resides in the people, and they have the ultimate verdict in any business of government.

SECTION 2 - BRANCHES OF GOVERNMENT

The Government of the Republic of Jayditaria is to be composed of three branches, to be called the “Executive”, the “Legislative”, and the “Judiciary”.

ECTION 3 - COMPOSITION OF THE EXECUTIVE BRANCH AND ITS PURPOSE

The executive branch is composed of a President, a Vice-President, a Cabinet composed of Ministers, ministries headed by Ministers from the Cabinet. This branch is responsible for approving and implementing laws passed by the legislative branch.

SECTION 4 - COMPOSITION OF THE LEGISLATIVE BRANCH AND ITS PURPOSE

The legislative branch is to be composed of a body called “Congress”, which will be bicameral, and is to be divided into two houses, called the “Senate”, composed of the Vice-President as President of the Senate and 5 senators, and the “House of Representatives”, which will be composed of 2 representatives from the 5 states, and a Speaker who is elected from such representatives. The duty of this branch is to create laws, and to impeach members of the other two branches.

SECTION 5 - COMPOSITION OF THE JUDICIAL BRANCH AND ITS PURPOSE

The judicial branch is composed of a Supreme Court, which is then composed of a Chief Justice, and two Associate Justices. The role of this branch is to investigate criminal offenses and decide whether a law is constitutional or not.

SECTION 6 - POWERS GRANTED TO ALL BRANCHES

All branches have the right to investigate and remove any member of the three branches, for any crimes and misdemeanors such member or members committed.

SECTION 7 - STATE GOVERNMENT

State governments shall be governed over by a mayor, who shall exercise executive duties. Its legislative branch shall be governed by the 2 persons who are members of the federal House of Representatives. Its judicial branch shall be governed by the Supreme Court.


Article 2 - National Territory

The national territory comprises the land owned by Jayditarian citizens, with all the islands and waters embraced therein, and all other territories over which Jayditaria has sovereignty or jurisdiction, consisting of its terrestrial, fluvial, and aerial domains, including its territorial sea, the seabed, the subsoil, the insular shelves, and other submarine areas. The waters around, between, and connecting the islands of the archipelago, regardless of their breadth and dimensions, form part of the internal waters of Jayditaria.


Article 3 - Citizenship

Section 1 - Description of Who Are Citizens of Jayditaria

Citizens of the Republic of Jayditaria include:

  1. People who have been citizens of Jayditaria before this Constitution,
  2. Mr. Jayden Riley V. Dagsa’s immediate family, his descendants, his close friends and their families, and;
  3. any person granted citizenship at the orders of the President of the Republic of Jayditaria or any person naturalized after an application process and the approval of the Jayditarian Ministry for Foreign Affairs.
